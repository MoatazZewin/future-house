about us

Future House

Future House Investment and Real Estate Development Company, owned by Engineer Romany Andraws, is a pioneering force in Egypt's real estate development sector. Established in 2008, our mission is to deliver luxurious, sustainable lifestyles through a diverse portfolio of projects.

With over 22 years of expertise in consulting and development, Future House has successfully launched and expanded numerous projects, including large-scale developments in new urban cities. Our portfolio encompasses residential, commercial, and administrative projects.

Future House has achieved remarkable success, executing over 150 projects comprising luxury apartments, villas, and commercial and administrative developments, such as Future Mall 1 and Future Mall 2. This showcases our company's long-standing independence and resilience.

Despite challenges facing Egypt's real estate market, we at Future House are confident in our ability to overcome them. Our distinguished team of experts, innovative design and execution approach, and commitment to excellence enable us to build not just properties, but a better future."
